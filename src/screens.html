The Journey

I went through every stage of training: medical school, residency, chief resident, cardiology fellowship, chief fellow, interventional fellowship, and now attending practice.

At every step, the biggest decisions weren't clinical — they were career decisions. What specialty to choose. How competitive I actually was. Which opportunities mattered. What contracts were worth signing. How to think about money early.

Most of the time, I was guessing — or relying on fragmented advice from people who meant well but didn't have the full picture.

HeartWise was built to fix that.

Every tool is based on real decisions I've made, physicians I've mentored, or mistakes I've watched others make. This isn't generic advice — it's a system to help you think clearly when it actually matters.
